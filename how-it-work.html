⚙️ How It Works

01

Teacher Generates QR

Teacher logs in and generates a unique, dynamic QR code for the specific class session.

02

QR is Time-Limited

The system applies a time-lock; the code expires automatically to prevent late scans or sharing.

03

Students Scan QR

Students use the built-in scanner to capture the code. Geolocation is verified at this stage.

04

Attendance Stored

Data is instantly transmitted and stored securely in the encrypted cloud database.

05

Reports & Analytics

Admin panels generate real-time visual charts and downloadable CSV/PDF reports.

06

Smart Alerts

Automatic SMS and Email notifications are sent for low attendance or successful check-ins.

07

Role-Based Secure Login

Customized portals for Admin, Faculty, and Students. Permissions are strictly enforced via JWT authentication to ensure data privacy.

08

Smart Bulk Import

Admins can upload entire student directories via CSV or Excel. The system automatically maps fields and generates unique IDs instantly.

09

Dynamic QR Generation

Teacher generates a unique, time-sensitive QR code. Every session refresh creates a new signature to prevent unauthorized reuse.

10

Advanced Security Layer

Beyond time-locks, we use Anti-Proxy Protection (Geofencing + Device Fingerprinting) to ensure students are physically in class.

11

Student Scan & Verify

Students scan through the encrypted app interface. The system cross-references GPS coordinates with the classroom's registered location.

12

Live Attendance Dashboard

Watch attendance tick up in real-time. Teachers see a live heat-map and "who's here" list as scans happen, with instant cloud syncing.

13

Reports & Smart Alerts

Automated PDF/CSV exports for Admins and instant SMS/Email triggers for students falling below the required attendance threshold.
